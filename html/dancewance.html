Precepts

1. Participants should bring their own material/props.
2. Performance can be solo or a group.
3. A Group may have 3-10 members.
4. All participants should provide their own music in pendrive and shall carry their own costumes.
5. Properties are optional and teams are free to choose their own properties.
6. No vulgar, obscene or explicit dresses, movements or songs will be entertained. Inclusion of any such disgusted issues would lead to disqualification.
7. Innovative ways of presenting any relevant messages are also encouraged and would lead to extra marks.
8. Time slot for each performance is 4 mins.
9. Decision of judges is final.


Classical

India has thousands of year old tradition of fine arts, classical and folk music and dances. Some of the world-famous dance forms that originated and evolved in India are Bharatnatyam, Kathak, Kathakali, Kuchipudi, Manipuri, Mohiniattam and Odissi.They were also effective in carrying forward the various mythological stories from generation to generation while entertaining the audiences.


Folk

Woven in rhythm, music and moments of folk dances are reflections of culture, traditions and community practices.they are a fervent and ecstatic celebration of life. Countries of the world have numerous folk dance forms wonderful expressed through traditional attires, vibrant music and choreography.


Tribal

Tribal dance is a type of dance used to symbolize the origin of one's connection with their culture. Every facet of society has a unique version of a tribal dance.

Fusion Band

Fusion is an improvised lead-follow approach to dancing to any style of music that does not have a strictly defined dance aesthetic. It is a triad of connection between your movement,your partner's movement,and the music.Depending on the music and the dancers,fusion can mean creating a new dance style to unique music,or combining two or more established dance aesthetics into a single danceto reflect the sound of a song combining


Contemporary

Contemporary dance is a style of expressive dance that combines elements of several dance genres including modern, jazz, lyrical and classical ballet. Contemporary dancers strive to connect the mind and the body through fluid dance movements. Contemporary dance stresses versatility and improvisation, unlike the strict, structured nature of ballet. This dance genre is often done in bare feet. Contemporary dance can be performed to many different styles of music.
